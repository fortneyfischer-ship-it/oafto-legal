Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

OAFTO Analyst Bot ("the app") is a personal analytics tool built and operated by an individual creator for personal, non-commercial use. It is not distributed publicly and is only used by its owner and a small number of explicitly authorized creator accounts.

What data is accessed

The app connects to the TikTok API to read publicly-visible and account-level video performance data (such as view counts, likes, comments, and shares) for accounts that have explicitly authorized the app via TikTok's own login and consent screen.

How data is used

Data is used solely to generate performance summaries and content-trend insights for the authorizing account holder. Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data storage

Data is stored locally on the app owner's own device/server, is not made publicly accessible, and is retained only as long as needed to generate historical trend comparisons.

Revoking access

Any authorized account holder may revoke the app's access at any time through their TikTok account's connected-apps settings. Doing so immediately stops all further data access for that account.

Contact

For questions about this app or to request data deletion, contact the app owner directly.
